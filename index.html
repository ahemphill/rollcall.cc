Roll Call is a cycling social club dedicated to promoting camaraderie on the roads (and off) in and around San Francisco. We ride twice a week:

Tuesdays

San Bruno Mountain

  • 6:40am Ritual Coffee on Valencia
  • San Bruno Mountain summit
  • ~8:15am Blue Bottle at Heath

Thursdays

Marin Headlands

  • 6:40am Fell & Baker/Panhandle
  • Marin Headlands loop
  • ~8:15am Rapha Cycle Club
